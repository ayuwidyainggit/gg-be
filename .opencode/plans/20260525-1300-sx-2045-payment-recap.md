## Formula / Final Mapping Recap

Per `deposit_type` bucket:

- `summary_cash = SUM(cash)`
- `cheque_giro = SUM(cheque_giro)` atau `SUM(cheque_amount)` sesuai source row.
- `transfer = SUM(transfer)`
- `return_amount = SUM(return_amount)`
- `credit_debit = SUM(credit_debit)`
- `discount = SUM(discount)`
- `total_payment_balance = SUM(payment_balance)`
- `total_expense = SUM(expense)` dengan AR expense row sudah negative.

Expense rule:

- AR: `expense = -ABS(SUM(de.payment_amount))` pada row expense/export/recap source.
- AP: `expense = 0`.
- Jika grand/deduction total perlu dihitung dari recap: gunakan `paid_total + total_expense`, karena `total_expense` sudah negative.
